Part 1: Self-Awareness & Emotional Intelligence

1. I regularly reflect on my emotions and how they impact my decisions.
2. I can accurately read the emotions and needs of my team members.
3. I actively seek feedback about my leadership from others.

Part 2: Communication & Influence

5. I communicate my vision and expectations clearly to my team.
6. I actively listen to others without interrupting or judging.
7. I adapt my communication style to different audiences and situations.
8. I can influence others to support my ideas without using positional authority.

Part 3: Team Development & Empowerment

9. I delegate meaningful work and trust my team to deliver results.
10. I invest time in coaching and developing my team members.
11. I create a psychologically safe environment where people feel comfortable taking risks.
12. I recognize and celebrate individual and team accomplishments.

Part 4: Strategic Thinking & Decision Making

13. I regularly think about long-term implications of today's decisions.
14. I make decisions based on data and analysis, not just intuition.
15. I involve the right people in decision-making processes.
16. I can shift course when new information suggests a better path.

Part 5: Accountability & Results

17. I hold myself and others accountable to commitments and standards.
18. I address performance issues promptly and constructively.
19. I set clear, measurable goals and track progress regularly.
20. I take ownership of mistakes and focus on solutions rather than blame.
